No commitment - Set up in minutes

Simplicity and transparency.

  • STEP 1

    You fill out the brief

    Select the type of video (Reels, YouTube, promo, etc.) Specify the goal, preferences regarding style, music, tempo. We quickly understand the task and prepare for editing.

  • STEP 2

    You send us the materials

    Video, photos, logo, brand guide (if available), examples of videos you like. Technical specifications (optional). We gather everything we need to get started without delay.

  • STEP 3

    The first draft

    We edit the first version of the video, deliver it to you in a way that is convenient for you. You evaluate the concept and rhythm and provide initial feedback.

  • STEP 4

    Feedback & Corrections

    If necessary, you leave comments or a list of changes (Google Docs/Notion), we make corrections (up to 2 rounds). Refine the video to the desired result.

  • STEP 5

    Final result

    We export it in the format you need (MP4, MOV, etc.), send it in a way that is convenient for you (Google Drive, WeTransfer, etc.) You receive a ready-to-publish file, and if necessary, we adapt the video for several platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube).
